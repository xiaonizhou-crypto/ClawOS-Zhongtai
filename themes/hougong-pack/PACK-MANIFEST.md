# Pack Manifest

## Pack name
- `hougong-pack`

## Purpose
A theme-expression pack for turning a neutral multi-agent organizational system into a structured inner-court / 后宫 governance language layer.

This pack is designed to:
- preserve the neutral kernel
- replace role language and presentation
- provide immersive but disciplined character voice
- support direct adaptation onto `sansheng`-style role systems

- authority framing
- identity / soul files
- avatar layer

## What this pack does not change

- approval logic
- task routing logic
- execution semantics
- internal business rules
